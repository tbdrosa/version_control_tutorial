Ingredients:
4 eggs 
(1) 12oz can of evaporated milk
(1) 14oz can of Sweetened condensed milk 
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 tsp corn starch 
A pinch of salt 

Recipe:
*Preheat oven to 350 degrees*

Part 1- Make the flan mixture
1. Mix eggs very well, about 1-2 mins
2. Add in evaporated milk, mix for 1 min
3. Add in sweetened condensed milk, mix for 1 min 
4. Add in vanilla extract, corn starch and salt, mix for 1 min 

Part 2 - Make the caramel syrup 
1. Mix 1/4 cup of water with 1 cup of sugar on medium heat (add sugar in slowly) 

Part 3 - 
1. Add caramel syrup to pan 
2. Then pour flan mixture in the same pan 
3. Get a larger pan and fill half way with water 
4. Sit the pan with the flan on top of the pan with the water in it
5. Put in the oven at 350* for 45-55 mins
